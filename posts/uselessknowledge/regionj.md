The potassium in a banana can emit small amounts of antimatter (positrons) that immediately decay. Not very useful to know. Read more about it [here](https://www.symmetrymagazine.org/2009/07/23/antimatter-from-bananas). Learn about ionizing radiation from [this](https://xkcd.com/radiation/) useless graph.
